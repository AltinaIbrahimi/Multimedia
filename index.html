How to play CARD GAME

This game is a great card game ! Younger players are often able to compete with older players because they can be very good at memory-based games. The objective is to collect the most pairs of cards. If they successfully match a pair they get to keep the cards, and that player gets another turn. When a player turns over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again (in the same position) and it becomes the next playerâ€™s turn. The trick is to remember which cards are where. The person with the most pairs at the end of the game wins. So, let's have fun with this cool GAME !
